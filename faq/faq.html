Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is VSSUT Hackathon an independent Event or an event of annual tech fest of VSSUT Burla?

VSSUT Hackathon is a part of techno-management fest E-Summit, VSSUT

2. Do I need to pay a registration fee?

For Hackathon, nope! But there are minimal charges according to the events in the Fest.

4. Is there a travel reimbursement?

Till now, we don't have provisions for reimbursing your travel costs. We may reimburse it in future depending upon the sponsorships we receive. So stay tuned!

5. What should I bring with me?

A laptop is all you need.

6. How to apply for VSSUT Hackathon?

Our registration process starts around this New Year. Stay Tuned!

7. What is the last date of registration?

20th January, 2018

8. How are applications evaluated?

The applications are evaluated on basis of your profile you have submitted to us. As we have fixed number of seats, we will review your applications on first come first serve basis.
